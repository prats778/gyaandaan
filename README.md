# Project-
### `THEME : GYAANDAAN`

`Project- Gyaandaan` is a user interactive platform that enables students to find a perfect mentor, chat with them and discuss their doubts over a video-conference. <br/>

### Features of Our Project!
---
<ol>
    
<li>Student Mentor Match & Searching Mentors: Mentees can search for mentors according to subject and time filters and tags.
</li></br>
<li>One-to-One chat: One-to-one mentor mentee chat for individual doubt clarifications and personalized support.</li></br>
<li>Discussion Forum: Discussion forum as a public chat room accessible to all mentees for communication and discussion.</li></br>
<li>Mentors Connect: Mentors can befriend their fellow mentors by adding them to their connection.
</li></br>
<li>Online Video-Conferencing platform: A embedded platform where mentors and mentees can meet for teaching and doubt sessions.
Notifications: Messages, sessions and assignment notifications here!
</li></br>
<li>Schedule Booking for Mentors: Mentors can flexibly fix their schedule according to their available day and time.</li></br>
<li>Progress Track: Keeping a check on their own progress for mentees.
</li></br>
<li>Notifications: Messages, sessions and assignment notifications here!
</li></br>
</ol>
